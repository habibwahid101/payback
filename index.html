Terms & Policy · শর্তাবলী

Last Updated: June 2025

1. Purpose of This Portal

This portal ("Payback") is a voluntary repayment initiative by Anisul Islam Chowdhury to track and process return claims from individuals who have previously invested with him or his associated businesses. This is not a legally mandated process.

2. No Legal Filing Acknowledgment

By submitting a claim, you acknowledge and confirm that you have NOT filed any legal case or complaint in any court, tribunal, or law enforcement authority against Anisul Islam Chowdhury or any of his associated businesses in relation to your investment. If any legal action is filed or is already pending, this repayment process shall be suspended for your claim until the matter is resolved.

3. Voluntary Process

This repayment is made in good faith on a voluntary basis. It is not an admission of any legal liability. Payments will be made in installments at a percentage determined by the portal administrator based on available capacity.

4. Claim Verification

All submitted claims are subject to verification. Anisul Islam Chowdhury reserves the right to approve, reject, or request additional information for any claim. False or fraudulent claims will be permanently rejected.

5. Payment Method

Payments will be made via the mobile banking number (Nagad/bKash) or bank account provided by the claimant. It is the claimant's responsibility to ensure the accuracy of payment details.

6. No Guarantee of Timeline

While every effort will be made to process payments as quickly as possible, no specific timeline is guaranteed. Payment schedules depend on available financial capacity.

7. Privacy

Your submitted information will be used solely for processing your claim and will not be shared with third parties without your consent.

8. Acceptance

By checking the agreement box and submitting your claim, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to all of the above terms.
